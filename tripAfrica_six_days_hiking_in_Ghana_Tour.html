Experience Six (6) Days Hiking In Ghana

OVERVIEW

Here at TripsAfrica, we treat our clients to a 10 day fun-packed trip of exploring the most amazing parts of Northern Ghana. This is a good amount of time to explore and experience the rich northern culture. You will also have the opportunity to experience wildlife and nature of this beautiful country called Ghana

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Kakum National Park
  • Shai Hills Game Reserve
  • Aburi Botanical Gardens
  • Boti Waterfalls
  • Umbrella Rock
  • Mount Afadjato
  • Tagbo Falls

Side Attraction

  • Cape Coast Castle
  • Accra City Tour

Day #1. Arival In Ghana

Upon arrival at the Terminal 3 of Kotoka International Airport, Accra-Ghana, You will be met by a staff and sent to your hotel.
Overnight in Accra

Day #2. Accra - Cape Coast - Accra

After breakfast, you will begin your tour with a visit to the Central Region of Ghana. If the hustle and bustle of Accra mars the traffic free tour you intend to have in Ghana, wait till you leave that behind as you enter a new and calmer region where you will see the rapid transformation. Your first stop will be to explore the Kakum National Park, one of the West Africa surviving tropical forest.

There is a canopy walkway which lies on the forest of Kakum and is the most common activity in the park. You will take a hike into the forest with the guidance of an experience park guide in addition to the canopy walkway experience. Depart afterwards to Accra but make a quick stop at the Cape Coast Slave Castle which was built in 1653 by the Swedish and was later taken over by the British. This castle played a significant role in the infamous transatlantic slave trade. Return back to Accra for your overnight

Day #3. Aburi Botanical Garden - Shai Hills - Akosombo

Breakfast will be served and then you will checkout from the hotel to start todayâ€™s tour. Your first stop will be to the Aburi Botanical Gardens. The cool mountainous weather and beautiful landscape of this area makes it an interesting destination for tourist who love the cool side of life. Upon arrival at the garden, you will be welcomed by the beautifully lined-up royal palm trees (Roystonea Regia) on both sides of the entrance leading to the garden. After your tour of the garden, you will depart to Shai Hills Game Reserve.

This reserve is covered with grassland and low dry forest vegetation. The reserve can boast of newly four hundred(400) plant and animal species including antelopes, bats, birds, baboons, zebra and many more. You will enjoy a hike into the reserve to familiarize yourself with some fauna and flora and also the beautiful rocks climbing.
Depart afterwards to Akosombo for your overnight.

Day #4. Akosombo - Boti Waterfalls

After an early morning breakfast, you will depart to Boti Waterfalls. Upon arrival, you will hike to the waterfall. You will also have the opportunity to hike to the Umbrella Rock and the three-headed-palm tree. Depart afterwards back to Akosombo to enjoy a cruise on one of the biggest man-made lakes in the world. Your cruise will take you to the longest bridge in Ghana. Return to your hotel afterwards to relax at your hotel.
Overnight in Hohoe.

Day #5. Hohoe - Liati Wote

After a restful breakfast, you will depart to have your last hike onto the highest mountain in Ghana, Mount Afadjato(885m). The mountain is located at Liati Wote, a village in the Volta Region of Ghana. The hike to the top of this mountain gives you a beautiful view of Ghana and Togo. You will also visit the Tagbo Waterfalls close to Mount Afadjato.
Return back to Hohoe for your overnight.

Day #6. Hohoe - Accra

Checkout after breakfast to Accra. Upon arrival, you will have lunch and visit one of the biggest art market in Ghana; Accra Arts Center. After your tour of this market, you will be transferred to the airport to depart home.
End of tour.
