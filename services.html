Our Services

TourGuiding

We provide for clients, professional, affluent and friendly tour-guides with several years of experience and matured interpersonal relationship skills. All our tour guides speak English and have a well cultured demeanour. We also have available multi-lingual tour-guides as well. Dotted across all tourist sites in Ghana and other countries we operate, these tour-guides are indigenes of the various towns where these tourist sites are located, thus, have first-hand detailed information and knowledge about these places. They stick with clients throughout their tours, answering questions and providing any assistance to clients whatever they may be.

Accomodation Services

Tripafrica Tours have contract agreement with accommodation facilities. Our client’s budget and comfort are our priorities and this make us provide the best of accommodation to meet our client’s preferences. We offer a wide range of accommodation booking services including;

  • 1-5 Star Hotels
  • Guesthouse
  • Suites and Apartment
  • Budget Hotels
  • Home-stays

Air Ticket Services

We have a special relationship with the two major domestic airliners in Ghana; African World Airlines (AWA) and PassionAir. We offer a wide range of their air travel services and reservations which include;Travel destinations to over five (5) different locations across Ghana and other West African countries.
We also provide international ticketing services which includes;

  • Scheduled tickets at IATA regulated rates
  • Special airline discounted fares on scheduled bookings using our special net-fare agreements with the airlines.

Airport Services

We offer and facilitate Airport transfer services in all Airports in Ghana, Togo and Benin. These services include;

  • Meet and Greet: a chauffeur-driven vehicle will meet and take you directly to your hotel or resort, while ensuring your safety and comfort.
  • Direct group transfer: a private mini-bus or coach is provided for clients traveling in groups. This service ensures all clients travel together while continuing their fun and it is significantly more economical than traveling in multiple vehicles.
  • Visa on arrival: We facilitate the visa on arrival process for our clients to ensure their stay in the country stress-free.
  • VIP Services

Car Rentals

Tripafrica Tours offers its clients professional car rental services while delighting them to a high standard personalised experience. Our esteem clients are picked up from their location by our professional English speaking drivers with a vehicle that suits their interests and provides ease and comfort. Our vehicles includes luxurious SUV’s, Scoda, Coaches and Mini buses for clients traveling in groups, families and also Safari Jeep with a top hatch for easy game viewing.

VISA Processing

We assist clients in acquiring tourist visas to Ghana, Dubai, Kenya, Ethiopia, Togo and Benin by facilitating and expediting the visa application processes. We also facilitate visa on arrival in countries that allow that.
