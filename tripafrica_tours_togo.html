Togo

TripAfrica Tours

Togo is a beautiful French speaking West African country and one of the smallest countries on the African continent with a population of about 8 million people. It is bordered by Ghana to the west, Benin to the East, Burkina Faso to the North and the Gulf of Guinea to the south. As a former French colony, its official language is French, however, other indigenous languages include Ewe, Mina, Kotokoli and Kabiye. The largest religious group is the Voodoo while a significant number of Christian and Muslim minorities.

Togo is tropical sub-Saharan nation which gives the country a relatively warm and friendly climate throughout the year. The country is famous for its flexible trade agreements among neighbouring countries making it a trading hub for West African countries. Further, being a nation on the Gulf of Guinea with a tropical climate, Togo is known for its palm-lined sandy beaches, a beautiful sight to look forward too. The country is also attractive for its colonial French infrastructure and beautiful hilltop villages.
